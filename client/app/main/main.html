WON’T PEOPLE STEAL IDEAS?

It would be the same as going on T.V. or taking out an ad to promote your idea. We do offer some layers of protection within our Terms of Use. We also offer a fixed date and time stamp to give you proof of when you created the idea. However, nothing is more secure than a patent and even those aren’t perfect. If you have something that is truly proprietary, patentable, and you have every intention of filing for that patent, we recommend you keep the “secret sauce” to yourself.

HOW DO MY IDEAS GET EXPOSURE?

IS THERE A COST FOR BEING A MEMBER?

Nope, Basic membership is free! Commercial membership, however, has a monthly fee. We gotta pay the bills somehow.
